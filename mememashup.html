Meme Mashup

I made a meme mashup of a video game meme and a cat meme. The origins of the video game meme come from Australia's rating system which labeled a fairly innocent video game "high impact sexual violence" which is as high a rating as they can give. Among the variations is a "low impact consensual cuddling" edit that I have combined with a cute cat meme. Now the meaning is an intangible urge to cuddle cute kittens.
